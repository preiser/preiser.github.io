About Me

I am a photographer based in Hendersonville, NC.

While the majority of my work has been for private and residential commission, I also work regularly with editorial and commercial clients to realize their photographic vision. My style and attitude is natural and straight-forward. I strive to create strong and unique images that stand on their own.

I also co-own the WNC Acupuncture clinic with my lovely and fearless wife Kristine Preiser, and we live in Hendersonville with our two year old daughter and eight year old Shanti-dog.
